Mattworld is a fixed monthly hardcore RimWorld challenge.

Every month gets a shared setup based on the current year and month. The start is harsh, the difficulty is high, and everyone gets the same basic run.

What to expect:

- Naked Brutality
- Randy Random
- Losing is Fun at 500 percent
- a full 100 percent world
- tougher pawn setup and start conditions

There are no comfort settings here. It is meant to be unfair, repeatable, and painful in a way you can compare with other players.
